Who are we?

Mkozi is a youth motivated start-up that intends to enable the youth particularly university students create alternative income streams.

Mkozi intends to do so by connecting university students to clients who need shopping and other tasks done for them.

  • Our Vision: We intend to mitigate cases of promiscuity and prostitution among the youth of Uganda by creating alternative income sources.
  • Our Mission: Our company's mission is to create alternative income streams for the youth. We continuously strive to add value to our agents and enable them to earn from their practical skills.

Why Choose Mkozi?

Mkozi through our values strives to deliver quality services to our cusomers efficiently.
